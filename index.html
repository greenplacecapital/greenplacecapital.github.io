About

We view Green Place as a vehicle for compounding our personal capital, alongside our investors.

The fund has no management fee, a high water mark, and charges a performance fee on profits that exceed a 6% compounding hurdle, modelled after the original Buffet partnerships.

JC van der Walt is the managing partner and portfolio manager.

Strategy

We own interests in a small number of businesses with attractive prospects, invested management, and sensible valuations.

We look for value, without constraints.
